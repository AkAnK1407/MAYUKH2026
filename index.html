About Us

Mayukh

Mayukh is the annual technical fest of Banasthali Vidyapith, celebrating the infinite realms of innovation and creativity. It serves as a gateway to countless dimensions of technology, imagination, and discovery. Through hackathons, workshops, competitions, and exhibitions, Mayukh brings together brilliant minds to explore uncharted territories of science and engineering. It is more than just a fest — it’s an experience that blurs boundaries, sparks curiosity, and empowers students to create their own universe of possibilities.
